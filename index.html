About

Located near Anusarn Market in Chiang Mai, Thailand, Miguel Art Tattoo is a professional studio welcoming both local clients and international visitors. The studio specialises in clean, detailed tattoo work with a strong focus on precision and long-lasting quality.

From fine line and blackwork to fully custom designs, each piece is developed through careful consultation to ensure balance, placement, and meaning. Every tattoo is crafted to be bold, clear, and built to age well.

Walk-ins are welcome, and appointments are available for larger or custom projects. Strict hygiene standards and a professional studio environment ensure every client feels confident and comfortable from start to finish.

Visiting Chiang Mai? Stop by for a consultation or message ahead to secure your booking.
