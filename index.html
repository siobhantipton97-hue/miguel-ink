About Us

Located near Anusarn Market in Chiang Mai, Thailand, Miguel Art Tattoo is a professional studio welcoming both local clients and international visitors.

We specialise in clean, detailed tattoo work with a strong focus on precision and long-lasting quality. From fine line and blackwork to fully custom designs, each piece is carefully developed through consultation.

Strict hygiene standards, professional equipment, and a calm studio environment ensure every client feels confident from start to finish.
